Subscriptions

Recurring revenue, handled.

From simple monthly plans to complex usage-based billing, RoadPay manages the full subscription lifecycle. Trials, upgrades, downgrades, cancellations, pauses -- all with a single API.

  • Metered, tiered, flat-rate, and per-seat pricing models
  • Automatic proration on plan changes
  • Configurable retry logic with exponential backoff
  • Dunning emails that actually recover revenue (38% avg recovery)
  • Webhooks for every lifecycle event
// Create a subscription in 3 lines
const subscription = await roadpay.subscriptions.create({
  customer: 'cus_abc123',
  plan: 'growth_monthly',
  trial_days: 14,
  payment_method: 'pm_card_visa'
});

// subscription.status === 'trialing'
// subscription.current_period_end === '2026-04-04'

Fraud Prevention

Block fraud. Keep customers.

Every transaction runs through our ML scoring pipeline. Combine Stripe Radar with custom rules you define. Adaptive thresholds learn your business and reduce false positives over time.

  • Real-time risk scoring on every payment attempt
  • Custom rule builder: block by country, velocity, amount, device
  • 3D Secure challenge flow handled automatically
  • Device fingerprinting and behavioral analytics
  • Chargeback protection and dispute management
  • Manual review queue for edge cases
// Custom fraud rule
await roadpay.rules.create({
  name: 'High-value new customer',
  conditions: [
    { field: 'amount', op: '>', value: 500_00 },
    { field: 'customer.age_days', op: '<', value: 7 }
  ],
  action: 'review' // 'allow' | 'review' | 'block'
});

// Flags for manual review, not auto-decline

FAQ
Common questions.

How is RoadPay different from using Stripe directly?

RoadPay is built on Stripe's infrastructure but bundles features that Stripe charges separately for: subscription management, fraud ML, invoicing, and revenue analytics. You get one integration instead of assembling pieces. Plus, we offer a self-hosted option Stripe never will.

Can I migrate from Stripe / PayPal / Square?

Yes. Our migration tool imports customers, payment methods, and active subscriptions. Most migrations complete in under an hour with zero downtime. We handle the re-tokenization.

What happens if I exceed my plan's volume?

Nothing breaks. You'll get a notification and we'll suggest upgrading. We never block transactions. You just pay the next tier's per-transaction rate on overages until you upgrade.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No. Month-to-month on all plans. Cancel anytime. Enterprise plans can optionally lock in annual pricing for a discount, but it's never required.

Do you handle tax calculation?

Yes. Built-in tax calculation for 40+ jurisdictions including US sales tax, EU VAT, and Canadian GST/HST. Automatic tax ID validation for B2B reverse charges.

What about chargebacks?

Our fraud prevention reduces chargebacks significantly. When they do happen, we provide automated evidence submission and dispute management. Business and Enterprise plans include chargeback protection insurance.
